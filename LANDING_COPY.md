## Landing Page Copy & Product Copy Assets

This document contains the main marketing copy, product messaging, and launch content used for the AI Spend Audit platform.

1. Landing Page Hero Section

The landing page is designed to immediately communicate the problem of unnecessary AI spending and how the platform helps solve it.

Main headline:
"Stop Double-Paying For Your Team's AI Tools"

Supporting subheadline:
"Run a free 60-second audit on your company's AI stack and discover wasted spending across tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, and Copilot."

Primary CTA:
"Run Free Audit"

Trust message:
"No credit card required. Shared reports are fully anonymized."

2. Tool Guidance & User Education

The platform includes small contextual tooltips to help users understand pricing overlaps and unnecessary subscriptions.

Examples:

ChatGPT Team plans are often unnecessary for very small teams.
Claude Team becomes expensive for teams under the minimum seat requirement.
Cursor Pro can replace multiple standalone AI coding subscriptions.
GitHub Copilot may become redundant if developers already use Cursor.

These explanations help users better understand the recommendations generated by the audit engine.

3. Product Hunt Launch Messaging

The Product Hunt positioning focuses on helping startups reduce AI subscription waste.

Launch tagline:
"Stop overpaying for ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor & Copilot."

The founder message explains how many companies subscribed to multiple AI tools during the AI boom and are now paying for overlapping services they no longer fully need.

The launch also highlights:

Free usage
Fast audit generation
Shareable CFO-style reports
No database or sensitive integrations required
