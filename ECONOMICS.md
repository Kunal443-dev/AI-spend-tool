Business Economics & Unit Margins

The AI Spend Audit platform is designed to operate with very low infrastructure costs. Hosting is handled using services like Render or Heroku, while MongoDB Atlas manages database storage. Since each audit stores very little data and uses lightweight AI processing through the Gemini API, the estimated processing cost per audit is extremely low (around $0.00015), resulting in very high profit margins.

The platform mainly follows a Product-Led Growth (PLG) approach, so customer acquisition cost stays close to zero. Growth comes organically through public report sharing and social screenshots. Revenue is generated by converting users into premium consulting or enterprise optimization services. With an estimated 2.5% conversion rate and an average service value of around $200, the estimated lifetime value of a lead is roughly $5.

For monetization, the platform can follow two approaches. The first is a freemium SaaS model where users get basic audits for free, while premium users pay for advanced features like tracking, integrations, alerts, and exports. The second approach is a lead-generation model where the tool remains free and qualified enterprise leads are referred to SaaS procurement or contract negotiation companies for commission or referral fees.
