## AI Spend Audit - Pricing Catalog

This document contains the pricing data used by the AI Spend Audit system. These values are referenced inside the backend pricing configuration and audit rules.

Supported AI Tools & Plans
ChatGPT (OpenAI)

Includes Free, Plus ($20/month), Team ($30/month with minimum 2 seats), and Enterprise plans.

Claude (Anthropic)

Includes Free, Pro ($20/month), Team ($30/month with minimum 5 seats), and Enterprise plans.

Cursor

Offers Hobby (Free), Pro ($20/month), and Business ($40/month) plans for AI-assisted coding.

GitHub Copilot

Provides Individual ($10/month), Business ($19/month), and Enterprise ($39/month) plans.

Gemini (Google)

Includes Free, Advanced ($20/month), Business ($20/month), and Enterprise ($30/month) plans with Google Workspace integrations.

Pay-as-you-go APIs

Supports flexible API-based pricing from providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini based on token usage and developer consumption.

The pricing catalog is used by the audit engine to calculate estimated spending, detect pricing inefficiencies, and generate optimization recommendations.
