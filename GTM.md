## GTM Strategy

This document explains the launch, growth, and customer acquisition strategy for the AI Spend Audit platform.

1. Target Audience & Value Proposition

The platform mainly targets startup founders, CTOs, finance teams, and agency owners who are spending heavily on AI tools and subscriptions.

The main goal of the product is to help teams quickly identify unnecessary AI expenses, overlapping subscriptions, and unused seats.

Core positioning of the product:

"Audit your AI subscriptions in under 60 seconds and discover potential yearly savings."

2. Product Launch Strategy

The initial launch strategy focuses on communities like Product Hunt and Hacker News.

For Product Hunt, the platform will be presented as a free AI cost optimization tool that helps companies identify duplicate spending across tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, and Copilot. The launch will highlight clean UI screenshots, savings dashboards, and benchmarking visuals.

For Hacker News, the focus will be more technical and logic-driven. The launch post will explain how the audit engine detects inefficient subscriptions using rule-based analysis instead of generic AI suggestions.

3. Product-Led Growth (PLG)

The application is designed with built-in viral sharing features.

After completing an audit, users receive a public shareable report link that can be shared on platforms like LinkedIn, X (Twitter), or inside company Slack channels. These reports are sanitized and do not expose sensitive company information.

The platform also includes a screenshot-friendly mode that allows users to easily share their savings reports on social media, helping drive organic traffic and visibility.

Every shared report promotes the platform with a small CTA encouraging others to run their own audit.

4. Content Marketing Strategy

Growth will also come through educational content around SaaS and AI cost optimization.

The platform can publish articles and guides explaining topics like:

Reducing duplicate AI subscriptions
Comparing AI developer tools
Optimizing team AI spending
Understanding SaaS pricing inflation

Lead emails collected through the platform can later be used for newsletters, pricing alerts, and enterprise consulting outreach.
